# Sky River Lodge

> Your Rustic Mountain Escape in the Cascades

## About

Nestled along the scenic Skykomish River in **Baring, Washington**, Sky River Lodge offers a perfect blend of rustic charm and modern comfort. Our cozy cabins provide an ideal retreat for those seeking to reconnect with nature while enjoying the convenience of home.

Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway, a family adventure, or a peaceful solo retreat, our lodge offers breathtaking mountain views, access to world-class hiking trails, and the tranquil sounds of the river right outside your door.

Located just an hour from Seattle, Sky River Lodge is your gateway to the stunning North Cascades wilderness. Experience the magic of the Pacific Northwest in every season.
